Aurora Finance — Fintech Rebrand

Aurora Finance came to us with a functional but forgettable product and a signup flow that was leaking users before they ever saw the app. The brief: make the marketing site feel as trustworthy and considered as the product itself, without slowing anything down.

We rebuilt the site around a single clear promise, restructured the signup path to remove every unnecessary step, and replaced the generic dashboard screenshots with a guided walkthrough that shows the product doing its job. Page-to-page navigation uses native browser transitions, so moving from the homepage into a feature page feels continuous rather than like a reload.

Result: signup conversion up 38% in the first six weeks, with no increase in support tickets and a Lighthouse performance score of 97.
